Fat Loss Nutrition Principles

Our fat loss meals are designed by certified nutritionists to create a calorie deficit while maintaining proper nutrition. Each meal contains:

  • High-quality lean proteins (20-30g per meal)
  • Complex carbohydrates for sustained energy
  • Healthy fats for hormone regulation
  • Plenty of fiber-rich vegetables
  • Portion-controlled calories (400-500 kcal per meal)

All meals are prepared with minimal processing and no artificial additives to support your health goals.
