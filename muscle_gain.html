Muscle Gain Nutrition Principles

Our muscle gain meals are crafted by expert nutritionists to support your muscle building goals. Each meal is designed to provide:

  • Adequate protein (1.6-2.2g per kg of body weight)
  • Complex carbohydrates for energy during workouts
  • Healthy fats for joint health and hormone production
  • A variety of vitamins and minerals for overall health
  • Strategic calorie intake to support muscle growth

All meals are prepared with high-quality ingredients and are free from artificial additives to ensure you get the best nutrition for your workouts.
