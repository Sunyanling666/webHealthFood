Sugar Control Nutrition Principles

Our sugar control meals are scientifically designed to help maintain healthy blood sugar levels:

  • Low Glycemic Index: All meals have GI less than 55 to prevent blood sugar spikes
  • Carbohydrate Management: 30-45g net carbs per meal with 10-15g fiber
  • Protein Priority: 20-30g lean protein per meal to slow glucose absorption
  • Healthy Fats: Monounsaturated fats from olive oil, nuts and avocados
  • Portion Control: Calorie-controlled meals (400-500 kcal) for weight management
  • Natural Sweeteners: Only use stevia or monk fruit when sweetness is needed

Developed in consultation with diabetes educators, our meals follow evidence-based nutrition guidelines while delivering exceptional flavor and satisfaction.
